Label stock refers to high-performance self-adhesive materials specifically designed for labeling applications. These versatile stocks offer customizable adhesive options, including water-based adhesive, hotmelt adhesive, and solvent acrylic, allowing adjustment according to the specific requirements of the application. In today's fiercely competitive environment, meeting customer needs is paramount, driving the continuous pursuit of excellence and innovation. It is imperative to exceed customer expectations and constantly explore new avenues to deliver the highest level of service and satisfaction. With label stock, businesses can rely on dependable adhesion and durability for various labeling needs, ensuring quality and consistency in product presentation and information dissemination.

Details Label Stock

  • Waterproof & Weatherproof Labels:
    Coated with synthetic or laminated layers and used for beverage, chemical, and outdoor applications.
  • Security & Tamper-Proof Labels:
    Includes holograms, destructible vinyl, and VOID labels and used in anti-counterfeit packaging and warranty seals
  • Material/Finish:
    Include details such as whether the paper is uncoated, coated, matte, glossy, recycled, or has any special finish.
  • Unit Price or Cost Information:
    Record pricing details to help with cost analysis and inventory management.
  • Eco-Friendly & Recyclable Labels:
    Made from biodegradable or recycled materials and ideal for sustainable brands and organic products.
