Siliconized Release Liners

Siliconized release liners are backing materials coated with a layer of silicone on one or both sides. Our siliconized release liner is meticulously engineered to meet specific application demands. Customized in length, width, and thickness, they ensure optimal coverage and compatibility. Designed to withstand pressure and maintain integrity, they're reliable during handling. Temperature ratings ensure stability across environments. Elasticity and flexibility enable smooth adherence to substrates, even with irregular contours. These factors combine to deliver reliable performance in diverse applications.

Details Siliconized Release Liners

  • Labels & Stickers:
    Acts as a protective liner for self-adhesive labels and Ensures easy application and prevents premature adhesion.
  • Industrial Tapes & Adhesives:
    Used in double-sided tapes, foam tapes, and medical tapes and provides a stable backing to protect adhesives before use.
  • Medical & Hygiene Products:
    Used for bandages, wound dressings, and medical tapes and ensures sterility and easy application.
  • Specialty Packaging & Transfer Films:
    Used in flexible packaging, graphic films, and decals and prevents adhesives from sticking during storage and transportation.
